1. Connect your phone and computer to the same WiFi network.
2. Start the platform with run.bat on Windows or run.sh on Mac or Linux.
3. Open http://localhost:8080/admin on the computer and click Mobile access.
4. Scan the QR code with your phone camera and open the link.
5. On iPhone, tap Allow if the browser asks for motion permission, and keep the phone upright because older phones or browsers without gyroscope support may need to use a webcam instead.
